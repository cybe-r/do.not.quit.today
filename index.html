A Social Initiative, Driven By Technology

Download this project as a .zip file Download this project as a tar.gz file

Social Pressure

We all can sense the increasing social pressure around us. It could be peer pressure, academic pressure, professional pressure or socio-economic pressure but it could lead to depression, anxiety and so many other problems if it crosses a certain barrier.

The Vision

This project is to give voice to such issues, understand them by listening to those who are facing it and look for solution among ourselves.

The Technology

There are various ways to raise voice, we're going to use the internet which is the most powerful tool and widely accessible. Our plan is to generate a web interface.
